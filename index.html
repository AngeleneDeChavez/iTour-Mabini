About Mabini Batangas

Mabini, Batangas, is a scenic coastal municipality in the Philippines, renowned for its natural beauty and diving spots. Located on the Calumpang Peninsula by the Verde Island Passage, it boasts world-class dive sites, vibrant coral reefs, and diverse marine life, making it a paradise for divers and snorkelers. Anilao, a popular barangay within Mabini, is often considered one of the top diving locations in the country. Beyond diving, Mabini features stunning landscapes of Balayan and Batangas Bays, lush hiking trails, and coastal resorts. Named after the revolutionary hero Apolinario Mabini, this charming town is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.
